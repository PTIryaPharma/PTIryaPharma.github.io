About Us

Our Story

PT IRYA PHARMA, established in 1994 and located in Indralaya, South Sumatra, has been contributing to the national pharmaceutical industry for 30 years. With the support of 250 competent employees, the company has successfully developed more than 50 types of pharmaceutical products including injectable preparations, ointments, creams, tablets, intranasal products, and various other medicines registered with BPOM RI. The comprehensive production facilities consist of sterile and non-sterile production areas, beta-lactam and non-beta-lactam zones, equipped with airlock systems and strict environmental controls, along with a Quality Control unit that ensures the highest quality standards. Armed with ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management systems and ISO 14001:2015 for environmental management systems, the company is committed to its vision of becoming a trusted partner in supporting healthy living through pharmaceutical innovations. Through its mission to develop effective and safe pharmaceutical products, provide holistic healthcare solutions, promote research and innovation, and establish strategic partnerships, PT IRYA PHARMA continues to maintain high quality standards in every aspect of its production and services, supported by comprehensive facilities including raw material storage warehouses, utility systems, air handling systems, and waste management that meet international standards.
